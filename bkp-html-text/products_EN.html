Productos

The raw material used for our products are reused plastic bags, the crocheting and yarning processes are totally hand-made

We re-use plastic bags of all type, texture, and color that usually end up in the trash can. We also use milk bags, VHS tapes, cassette tapes, and wires. Each object is unique.

Through the sale of our products we aim to initiate a relationship with our clients, motivating them to consume responsibly and to be aware of the excessive use of plastic, encouraging them to implement solutions at the individual level.

NEW: TEJIENDO CONCIENCIA’S “DO-IT-YOURSELF” KIT

Buying this product you learn how to reuse plastic and how to crochet, you also collaborate with:

  • Reducing the number of plastic bags that arrive at the dumps and will never degrade completely.
  • Benefiting the women that make the plastic yarn collaborating with decreasing their social vulnerability.
