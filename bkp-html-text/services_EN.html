We offer services to schools, associations, and any other type of institution interested in creating awareness about the excessive use of plastic and in finding solutions together, implementing techniques of responsible consumption and techniques of solid waste management.

CONFERENCE AND DEBATE

We present the problem of plastics in a talk adapted for different audiences; for example, for elementary school, for high school or for community centers. We open a dialog to think over and identify habits that we could improve, and we co-create solutions with the public that can be implemented at the individual level.

Duration: 1 hour•Ages: From 8 years

PLASTIC CROCHET WORKSHOPS AND DEBATE

1. Initial

We teach how to cut and crochet plastic bags. We expose models of finished products and propose feasible projects in the short term. We open a dialog to think over and identify habits that could be improved, and we co-create solutions with the public that can be implemented at the individual level. Proposed for people of both genres with different crocheting abilities.

Duration: 2 hours•Ages: From 12 years•Quota: up to 20 people

2. Advanced

We teach how to cut and crochet plastic bags to achieve a finished product (e.g. owl mobiles, bird mobiles, baskets). We open a dialog to think over and identify habits that could be improved, and we co-create solutions with the public that can be implemented at the individual level. Proposed for people of both genres with intermediate crocheting abilities.

Duration: 4 hours•Ages: From 12 years•Quota: up to 10-20 people

3. Intensive

We conduct three meetings of 2 hours each, separated by a minimum of one week, where we teach how to cut and crochet plastic bags to finish a tote bag. We follow the crocheted products and recommend improvements to achieve a tidy product. We open a dialog to think over and identify habits that we could improve, and we co-create solutions with the public that can be implemented at the individual level. Proposed for people of both genres with different crocheting abilities.

Duration: 6 hours•Ages: From 12 years•Quota: up to 25 people
